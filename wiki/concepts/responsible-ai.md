# Responsible AI

Responsible AI refers to the practice of developing and deploying artificial intelligence systems that are safe, ethical, and beneficial to humanity while minimizing potential harms.

## Core Principles

- **Safety**: Ensuring AI systems behave predictably and do not cause harm
- **Fairness**: Avoiding discrimination and ensuring equitable outcomes across populations
- **Transparency**: Making AI decisions interpretable and explainable
- **Privacy**: Protecting individual data rights and confidentiality
- **Accountability**: Establishing clear responsibility for AI outcomes

## Governance Frameworks

- EU AI Act risk classification
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework
- OECD AI Principles
- Industry-specific regulations
