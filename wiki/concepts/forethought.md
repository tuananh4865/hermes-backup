# Forethought

**Forethought** is an AI customer service platform that pioneered self-improving AI agents. In March 2026, Zendesk completed the acquisition of Forethought to deploy its self-improving AI technology across Zendesk's customer service platform.

## Overview

Forethought's core innovation is the **"Resolution Learning Loop"** — a self-improving AI system that:
1. Detects workflow gaps in customer service interactions
2. Generates new procedures to address those gaps
3. Tests optimizations against benchmarks before deployment
4. Continuously learns and improves from real interactions

This represents one of the first production deployments of truly self-improving AI agents in enterprise customer service.

## Key Products

### Resolution Learning Loop

The proprietary technology that powers Forethought's AI agents. Unlike standard rule-based bots that require manual updates, the Resolution Learning Loop autonomously improves by:
- Analyzing failed interactions to identify patterns
- Generating candidate procedures automatically
- Validating improvements through A/B testing before full deployment
- Learning from each resolution to prevent future failures

### Integration with Zendesk

Post-acquisition, Forethought's technology integrates with Zendesk's suite:
- Ticket deflection and routing
- Automated response generation
- Knowledge base optimization
- Agent assist and suggestions

## Why Zendesk Acquired Forethought

The core value proposition cited in the acquisition: **self-improving AI that doesn't require manual configuration updates**. Traditional chatbots and automated systems break when they encounter novel situations. Forethought's agents adapt autonomously, reducing the operational burden of maintaining AI customer service systems.
