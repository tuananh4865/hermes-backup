# Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. (NASDAQ: AAPL) is a consumer electronics and software company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Known for iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and the Apple Silicon chip line.

## Key Products

- **iPhone** — Smartphones running iOS
- **Mac** — Desktop and laptop computers running macOS
- **iPad** — Tablets running iPadOS
- **Apple Watch** — Wearables running watchOS
- **Apple Silicon** — Custom ARM chips (M-series) for Mac
