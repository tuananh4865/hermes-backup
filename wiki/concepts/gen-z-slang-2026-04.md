# Gen Z Slang April 2026

## English Slang (Global TikTok)

| Term | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Chuzz** | Worse/uglier version of someone (NEW) | "That's my chuzz era photo" |
| **Gyatt** | Exclamation for impressive/remarkable | "Gyatt, that's actually huge" |
| **Rizz** | Natural charm/flirting ability | "He has mad rizz at every party" |
| **Brain rot** | Mental fog from too much content | "TikTok gave me serious brain rot" |
| **Sigma wolf** | Independent, no group chat needed | "He's full sigma wolf, no group chat" |
| **6 7** | Nonsense hype phrase/punchline | "Hit 6:07 on the jump — 6 7!" |
| **Lowkey/Highkey** | Subtly/obviously | "Lowkey obsessed with this track" |

## Vietnamese Slang (TikTok Vietnam)

### High-Frequency Terms

| Term | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Đỉnh / Đỉnh khoai** | Excellent, top-tier, awesome | "Bộ phim này đỉnh quá!" |
| **Toang** | Screwed, failed, something went wrong | "Quiz toang rồi" |
| **Xịn xò / Xịn** | Cool, sophisticated, impressive | "Bộ đồ này xịn xò quá" |
| **Chill** | Relax, laid-back | "Cuối tuần này chill thôi" |
| **Gato** | Jealous (from "jealous") | "Mình gato quá!" |
| **Gắt** | Intense, fierce, impressive | "Phong cách này gắt dữ" |
| **Lầy** | Naughty, playful (from "lầy" = muddy) | "Bạn đó lầy lắm" |
| **Cày** | Grind, study/work hard | "Cày exam nè" |
| **FA** | Forever Alone | "Mình FA mà" |
| **Phét** | Brag, exaggerate, talk big | "Đừng có phét" |
| **Jet jet / Jet Jet** | Pretending to be fancy (mocking) | "Mình jet jet thôi" |

### Vietnamese Internet Culture

| Term | Meaning | Context |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Thả thính** | Drop hints, flirt | Dating context |
| **Bóc phốt** | Expose, call out | Gossip culture |
| **Hóng hớt** | Gossip, hear rumors | From "hóng" (to listen) |
| **Ủa?** | Expression of surprise/disbelief | "Ủa, thật hả?" |
| **Ô trời** | Oh my god | From "ông trời" |
| **J dz tr** | "Gì vậy trời?" | Saigoneese texting style |
