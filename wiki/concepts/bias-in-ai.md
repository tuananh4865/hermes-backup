# Bias in AI

AI bias refers to systematic errors or unfair outcomes that arise from assumptions in machine learning algorithms, training data, or system design.

## Sources of Bias

- **Training Data Bias**: Datasets reflecting historical inequalities or skewed sampling
- **Label Bias**: Subjective or inconsistent labeling decisions
- **Algorithmic Bias**: Model architecture or objective functions that inadvertently favor certain groups
- **Deployment Bias**: Misapplication or overgeneralization in production contexts

## Types of Bias

- **Selection Bias**: Non-representative training data
- **Confirmation Bias**: Reinforcing pre-existing beliefs through data collection
- **Measurement Bias**: Flawed or inconsistent measurement across groups
- **Aggregation Bias**: Applying models uniformly across diverse populations

## Mitigation Strategies

- Diverse and representative data collection
- Bias auditing and fairness metrics
- Explainable AI techniques
- Inclusive design practices
