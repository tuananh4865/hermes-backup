# Base44

**Base44** is a vibe-coding platform founded by a solo developer (Shlomo) that enables non-technical users to build full-fledged applications using natural language. In June 2025, Wix acquired Base44 for **$80 million cash** — just 4-6 months after launch — making it one of the most successful examples of a one-person AI-powered software company.

## What is Base44?

Base44 is built on the concept of **vibe coding** — a development paradigm where users describe what they want in plain English, and the AI builds the entire application without writing code. The platform handles:

- UI/UX generation from natural language descriptions
- Backend logic and database schema creation
- API integrations and deployment

After Karpathy posted about vibe coding on X, users flocked to platforms like Replit, Lovable, Bolt, and Base44. Base44 stood out by targeting non-technical founders who wanted to validate business ideas without hiring developers.

## The $80M Acquisition

Base44 was founded in February 2025 and acquired by Wix in June 2025 — just 4 months later. The acquisition validated several key trends:

1. **Solo founders can compete with funded startups** — AI tools reduce the cost of building to near zero
2. **Vibe coding works** — natural language app generation has crossed the quality threshold for real businesses
3. **Acqui-hires in AI tooling are accelerating** — big platforms (Wix, Shopify, Salesforce) buying AI-native tools

## Why This Matters

Base44's success signals a shift in how software is built:
