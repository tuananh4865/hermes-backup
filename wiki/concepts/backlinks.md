# Backlinks

Backlinks are links pointing to the current page from other pages. They are the foundation of navigating bidirectional knowledge graphs.

## How It Works

When page A links to page B using `[[B]]`:
- Page A has an outgoing link to B
- Page B automatically shows a backlink pointing back to A

## Why It Matters

- **Discovery**: Find related content you forgot you wrote
- **Context**: See how ideas connect across the wiki
- **Research**: Trace a thought's origins through the graph

## Implementation

Most modern PKM tools handle backlinks automatically:
- [[Roam Research]] — Built-in backlink panel
- [[LogSeq]] — Backlinks in right sidebar
- Obsidian — Backlinks panel + graph view
- [[wiki]] — Backlink tracking via [[wiki-self-heal]]
