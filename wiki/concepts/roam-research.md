# Roam Research

Roam Research is a networked thought tool pioneered by Conor White-Sullivan. It introduced bidirectional links (双向链接) and daily notes as first-class concepts in personal knowledge management.

## Key Concepts

- **双向链接 (Bidirectional Links)**: When page A links to page B, page B automatically shows a backlink to page A
- **Daily Notes**: Each day gets a dedicated page for capturing thoughts
- **Block References**:引用单个段落而不是整个页面
- **Outliner**: Nested bullet structure for organizing thoughts

## Legacy

Roam Research popularized the concept of treating your notes as a graph rather than a hierarchy. It inspired many subsequent tools including [[LogSeq]], Obsidian, and Notion.
