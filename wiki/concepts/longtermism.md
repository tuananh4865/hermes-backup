# Longtermism

Longtermism is an ethical framework that prioritizes the long-term flourishing of future generations, arguing that the number of people who could exist in the future vastly outweighs present concerns.

## Overview

Longtermist thinking emphasizes existential risk reduction, sustainable development, and ensuring that future civilizations can thrive.
