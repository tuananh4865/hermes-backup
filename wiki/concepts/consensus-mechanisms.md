# Consensus Mechanisms

Consensus mechanisms are protocols that enable distributed systems to agree on a single state or value, critical for blockchain networks and decentralized applications.

## Types

- **Proof of Work (PoW)**: Miners compete to solve cryptographic puzzles
- **Proof of Stake (PoS)**: Validators lock tokens as collateral
- **Delegated PoS**: Elected validators confirm transactions
- **Byzantine Fault Tolerance**: Surviving nodes reach agreement despite failures
