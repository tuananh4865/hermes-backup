# Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO encompasses optimization activities performed outside a website—including backlinks, social signals, and brand mentions—to improve search rankings.

## Key Activities

- Building high-quality backlinks
- Social media engagement
- Brand mentions and citations
- Guest posting and influencer outreach
