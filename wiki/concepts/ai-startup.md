# AI Startup

## Overview

An AI startup is a company that leverages artificial intelligence as a core component of its product or service. In 2026, AI startups evolved from requiring large teams and significant capital to being buildable by solo founders using AI agent tools.

## 2026 Landscape

### Key Trends

1. **Solo Founders Winning:** AI enables one-person companies to compete with traditional startups
2. **AI-First Architecture:** Products built with AI agents as primary workers
3. **Lean Operations:** Small teams (1-5 people) shipping products that previously required 50+
4. **Rapid Shipping:** MVPs in days instead of months

### Funding Trends (January 2026)

- AI and Infrastructure remain top-funded sectors
- Seed rounds smaller but more numerous
- VC increasingly backing solo founders with AI leverage

## Business Models

### AI SaaS
Subscription-based AI tools with recurring revenue. Key metrics: MRR, churn, LTV.

### AI Agent Services
Building and deploying AI agents for enterprise clients. Higher touch but higher ACV.

### AI-Enabled Marketplaces
Platforms augmented with AI for matching, recommendations, automation.

### API-Based AI
Providing AI capabilities via API to other businesses.

## Tools for AI Startups

### Development
- Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub Copilot (coding)
- LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen (agent frameworks)
- Vercel, Netlify (deployment)

### Infrastructure
- Supabase, Firebase (backend)
- Stripe (payments)
- n8n, Zapier (automation)

### Customer Success
- Intercom, Crisp (support)
- Loom, Tella (async communication)

## Success Factors

1. **Ship Fast:** Use AI to iterate quickly
2. **Solve Pain:** AI is a tool, not a selling point
3. **Unit Economics:** AI reduces costs but discipline matters
4. **Distribution:** Use AI for marketing and outreach
5. **Customer Intimacy:** Solo founders excel at customer relationships
