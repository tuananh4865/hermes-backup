# One Person Unicorn

## Overview
"One-person unicorn" describes the emerging pattern of solo founders building billion-dollar businesses using AI agents, lean operations, and modern tooling. What once required large teams now can be accomplished by one person with the right AI workflow.

**Core insight**: AI agents replace employees. One motivated developer with AI tools can now do what previously required 10+ people.

## The Pattern

### 1. Lean Ops Stack
- **AI coding agents**: Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot for development
- **AI writing**: GPT-4o, Claude for content, docs, support
- **AI agents**: Autonomous agents for research, outreach, operations
- **Automation**: n8n, Zapier, Make for workflows
- **No-code**: Bubble, Webflow for frontend

### 2. Solo Founder Workflow (Vibe Coding)
```
Idea → AI generate code → Iterate → Ship → Scale
         ↓
    AI handles: backend, devops, research
    Human: product, design, marketing
```

### 3. Revenue Models

| Model | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| AI SaaS | Usage-based pricing | Claude API, Cursor |
| Affiliate | Commission on referrals | AI tool affiliates |
| Micro-SaaS | Niche tool for small market | Indie hacker tools |
| Info products | Courses, newsletters | ConvertKit, Gumroad |
| Services | AI-augmented consulting | Retainers |

## Success Factors

### What Works
- **Niche focus**: Dominate a small market before expanding
- **Speed to market**: Ship in days, not months
- **AI leverage**: Automate everything repeatable
- **Direct sales**: No intermediaries, own the customer relationship
- **Community**: Build audience before product

### What Doesn't Work
- **Competition with big tech**: Can't out-resource OpenAI, Google
- **Generic products**: Need specific positioning
- **Waiting for perfect**: Ship MVP, iterate from real feedback
- **Solo burnout**: AI helps but founder still does everything

## Examples in Wild

### MicroConf Speakers (Indie Hackers)
- Countries: US, EU, Southeast Asia
- Tools: Gumroad, Stripe, Ghost
- Revenue: $10K-$500K ARR
- Pattern: One-person operation, 100% digital

### AI Tool Affiliates
- Promote: Cursor, Claude Code, Vercel, Supabase
- Commission: 20-30% recurring
- Content: YouTube, Twitter, newsletters
- Scale: Audience building → passive income

### Solo SaaS Founders
- Typical stack: Next.js, Stripe, PostgreSQL, Railway
- Launch: Product Hunt, Hacker News
- Growth: SEO, word of mouth, communities

## Getting Started

1. **Pick a niche**: Small enough to dominate, large enough to monetize
2. **Validate before building**: Talk to 10 potential customers
3. **Ship fast with AI**: Use vibe coding to build MVP in 1-2 weeks
4. **Collect payments early**: Stripe, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy
5. **Build in public**: Twitter, YouTube, newsletters create audience
6. **Automate operations**: AI agents handle support, research, outreach
