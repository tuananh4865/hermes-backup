# Medvi

**Medvi** is a telehealth company that sells GLP1 weight loss treatments, built by a single founder (and his brother) using AI tools. It reached **$401 million in ARR in its first year** — making it the most prominent case study for the "one-person billion-dollar AI company" thesis as of April 2026.

---

## Overview

Medvi represents the practical proof-of-concept for the 2026 AI startup narrative: a solo or duo founder, leveraging AI coding tools (vibe coding), can build a capital-efficient, high-margin business in a regulated industry like telehealth.

**Key metrics:**
- **ARR:** $401 million (year 1)
- **Margins:** Surpassing incumbent competitors
- **Team size:** Primarily one person (founder) + brother
- **Category:** Telehealth, GLP1 weight loss medications
- **AI tools used:** Vibe coding workflow (AI-assisted development)

---

## Why Medvi Matters

Medvi is frequently cited as evidence that:

1. **AI amplifies solo founders** — One developer can now build what previously required a team of 10-20
2. **Capital efficiency has inverted** — AI tools mean the cost to build a working product is near zero vs. the $2-5M it might have cost in 2022
3. **Healthcare is AI-accessible** — Regulated industries (healthcare, legal, finance) are no longer excluded from solo-founder narratives
4. **GLP1 market is exploding** — The weight loss drug market (Ozempic, Wegovy, compounded GLP1) is projected to reach $100B+ by 2030

---

## Business Model

Medvi operates as a **telehealth platform** connecting patients with GLP1 prescriptions:

1. Patient completes online consultation
2. AI-assisted intake process (built with vibe coding)
3. Physician review via asynchronous workflow
4. Medication shipped directly to patient

The **AI advantage** is primarily in:
- Patient onboarding and intake automation
- Content and communication scaling
- Operational workflow automation
- Marketing and content creation
