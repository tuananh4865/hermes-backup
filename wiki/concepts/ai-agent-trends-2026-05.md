# AI Agent Trends May 2026

## Key Trends

### 1. Multi-Agent Systems Go Mainstream
- Google's prediction: multi-agent workflows mainstream in 2026
- Protocols like **A2A** (Agent-to-Agent) and **MCP** (Model Context Protocol) enable cross-vendor collaboration
- 80% of enterprise apps will have agents by 2026 (IDC)

### 2. "Agent Leap" — From Prompts to Autonomous Workflows
- The era of simple prompts is over
- AI now orchestrates complex, end-to-end workflows semi-autonomously
- Digital assembly lines: human-guided, multi-step workflows with multiple agents

### 3. Agentic AI Production Scaling
- Focus shifting from experimentation to production deployment
- On-device generative AI growing
- Autonomous workflow orchestration becoming standard

### 4. Human-Guided Hybrid Workflows
- Best results: hybrid = human oversight + autonomous execution
- Fully autonomous still struggles with edge cases
- Human-guided multi-step workflows showing 10x productivity gains
