The preference for auto-approval mode suggests a desire to minimize friction in preserving personal history, ensuring that every interaction is immediately available for review. Automating the saving of raw conversations aligns with a workflow where consistency and ease are paramount, allowing for deep focus on content creation without the cognitive burden of manual archiving. This approach supports a multi-layered knowledge system where both symbolic (wiki) and semantic (Obsidian) elements coexist for nuanced information retrieval.
