# Technological Forecasting

Technological forecasting uses systematic methods to predict how technology will evolve, informing strategic planning and research priorities.

## Methods

- Expert elicitation and Delphi methods
- Trend extrapolation and growth curves
- Scenario planning
- Morphological analysis
