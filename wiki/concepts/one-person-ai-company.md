# One-Person AI Company

The AI democratization narrative in 2026: solo developers shipping production SaaS with AI assistance, achieving "one-person billion dollar company" scale.

## Patterns

### Micro-SaaS with AI
- No-code/low-code + AI = rapid MVP shipping
- Local LLM + MCP = fully local, private AI stack
- AI agent services as freelance offerings

### Solo Developer Stack
- **Frontend**: Vibe coding (Cursor, Claude Code)
- **Backend**: Firebase/Supabase (no ops)
- **AI**: Local LLM (MLX/llama.cpp) + MCP
- **Deployment**: Vercel/Netlify (zero config)

### Revenue Models
- AI-powered SaaS subscriptions
- Affiliate marketing with AI automation
- AI consulting (agent implementation)
- AI-as-a-service APIs
