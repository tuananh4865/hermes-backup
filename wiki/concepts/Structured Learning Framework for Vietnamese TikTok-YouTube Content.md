# Structured Learning Framework for Vietnamese TikTok/YouTube Content

## Summary
This concept outlines a comprehensive research and compilation project aimed at creating structured learning materials (markdown format) focused on building successful TikTok and YouTube channels. The primary goal is to analyze the content writing style of top Vietnamese product-review creators to extract actionable strategies in areas like storytelling, viral content mechanics, affiliate marketing, and natural spoken-word content creation.

## Key Insights
- **Focus on Natural Language:** A critical component of the learning framework is studying "natural content writing" (văn nói), moving beyond formal written scripts to capture the authentic voice of successful Vietnamese creators.
- **Modular Learning Structure:** The project is organized into modular files (e.g., storytelling, affiliate, confidence) and a master index, facilitating structured study rather than just raw transcript consumption.
- **Creator Deep Dive Methodology:** The process involves identifying top creators and creating dedicated profile pages and analysis files for each, allowing for comparative analysis of their specific content formulas and successful tactics.

## Analysis
The project began by compiling existing TikTok transcripts and foundational learning materials related to general channel growth and affiliate strategies. The current phase shifts focus from general theory to deep-dive competitive analysis by identifying the top 10 Vietnamese product review creators across various niches (skincare, food, tech). This involved extensive web searching and downloading video transcripts to gather raw data for subsequent analysis.

The core methodology involves creating a dedicated directory structure for each creator, including profile pages and specific transcript analysis files (`phan-tich.md`). This structured approach ensures that the gathered content is not merely archived but is systematically broken down into actionable formulas and techniques related to storytelling and viral hooks. The ultimate aim is to synthesize these disparate creator styles into a unified "Vietnamese Review Content Formula."

Current progress indicates successful data collection for several creators, but technical roadblocks remain in fully downloading all video transcripts (specifically YouTube content) due to configuration errors with the `yt-dlp` tool. Future efforts will focus on overcoming these download hurdles and completing the analysis phase to generate comprehensive formula documents (`cong-thuc-viet-review.md`) that serve as a complete learning resource for aspiring Vietnamese content creators.
