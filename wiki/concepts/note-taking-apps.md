# Note-taking Apps

Tools for capturing, organizing, and retrieving knowledge. Categories span from simple outliners to complex knowledge graphs.

## Categories

### Outliners
- [[LogSeq]] — Open-source, local-first outliner
- Roam Research — Block-based outliner with bidirectional links
- Workflowy — Minimal infinite outliner

### Wikis
- [[wiki]] — This knowledge base (git-based, markdown)
- Obsidian — Local markdown with plugin ecosystem
- [[Notion]] — All-in-one workspace with databases

### Zettelkasten
- [[zettelkasten]] — Method for atomic, linked notes
- Digital implementations: Obsidian, LogSeq, Roam

### Daily Notes
- [[LogSeq]] — Daily journal as entry point
- Roam Research — Daily notes page
- Apple Notes — Quick capture (no linking)

## Choosing a Tool

Consider:
- **Local-first**: LogSeq, Obsidian (vs cloud: Notion, Roam)
- **Linking**: Bidirectional links (Roam, LogSeq, Obsidian) vs flat notes (Apple Notes)
- **Graph view**: Visual navigation of connections
- **API access**: Notion has full API, others limited
