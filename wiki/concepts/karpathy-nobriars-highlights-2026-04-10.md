## Summary

Karpathy's key insights from this interview:
1. **AI Psychosis is real** - The feeling of being bottlenecked by your own ability to direct agents
2. **Macro actions** - Move from single line code to delegating whole functionalities
3. **Personality matters** - Agents with good personality feel like teammates
4. **Multi-agent collaboration** - The future is multiple agents working together
5. **Education reshuffling** - Agents will handle routine learning, humans focus on few bits only they can provide
6. **Micro GPT** - Karpathy's 200-line minimal GPT implementation as his value add
