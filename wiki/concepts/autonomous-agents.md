# Autonomous Agents

Autonomous agents are AI systems that perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions independently to accomplish specific goals without requiring continuous human intervention.

## Overview

Building on classical [[intelligent agents]], modern autonomous agents leverage large language models to handle ambiguous, real-world tasks across diverse domains.
