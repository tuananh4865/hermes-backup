# Double Spending

Double spending is the risk that a cryptocurrency can be spent more than once, undermining the integrity of the digital currency system.

## Overview

Unlike physical cash where a dollar spent is gone, digital tokens can theoretically be duplicated. Blockchain consensus mechanisms prevent double spending through transaction ordering and cryptographic verification.
