# Autoresearch Karpathy

> An autonomous AI research agent system open-sourced by Andrej Karpathy in March 2026.

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy released AutoResearch in March 2026 — a 630-line Python system implementing a self-improving AI agent that autonomously designs, runs, and iterates on experiments using a small but real LLM training setup.

Shopify CEO Tobias Lütke called it "the future of research" after trying the system.

## Key Innovation

The agent isn't just executing tasks — it has access to training infrastructure and can improve its own capabilities by:
1. Designing experiments autonomously
2. Running experiments against a real LLM training setup
3. Analyzing results and iterating
