# Instruction Tuning

Instruction tuning is a fine-tuning technique that trains language models on pairs of instructions and desired responses, improving their ability to follow natural language directives.

## Overview

By training on diverse instruction-output examples, models learn to respond appropriately across a wide range of tasks and query types.
