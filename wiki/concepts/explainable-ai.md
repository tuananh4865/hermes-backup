# Explainable AI (XAI)

Explainable AI encompasses techniques that help humans understand, interpret, and trust machine learning model outputs.

## Why Explainability Matters

- Building trust in AI systems
- Meeting regulatory requirements (GDPR, EU AI Act)
- Debugging model errors
- Ensuring fairness and accountability

## Techniques

- **SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations)**: Game-theoretic feature importance
- **LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations)**: Local linear approximations
- **Attention Visualization**: Highlighting input regions driving model predictions
- **Counterfactual Explanations**: "What-if" scenarios showing decision boundaries
