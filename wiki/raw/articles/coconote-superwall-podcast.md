# Key Points

## Growth Timeline

| Timeline | Milestone |
|----------|-----------|
| 45 days | $100K ARR |

| 5 months | $2M ARR |

## TikTok Strategy: Quality Over Virality

**Core insight:**
> "We would rather have 10 million views on a video that converts well because it's actually targeting our target audience as opposed to a 40 million view video that is just unique and novel and drives low intent traffic."

**PDF to Brain Rot example:**
- 40M views
- Only 3,000 trial starts
- Converted to only $25K revenue
- Lesson: Virality ≠ revenue

## Navy Seals, Not Navy

Built team of **10-12 part-time creators** (not influencers):
- Understood product deeply
- Understood audience deeply
- Quality over quantity

## Trojan Horse Strategy

Use **novel/fun content** to get users in the door, then convert to real product.

## App Framework

Apps that succeed cover TWO things:
1. **Repeatable use case**
2. **Core to identity**

Then add **wow factor** on top.

## AI App Principles

1. What's possible today that wasn't possible 6 months ago?
2. Build something attached to identity
3. Build repeatable habits
4. Focus on learner outcomes, not just features
