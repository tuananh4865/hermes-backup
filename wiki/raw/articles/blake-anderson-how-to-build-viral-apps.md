# Key Principles

1. **Solve Big Problems** — Maslow's hierarchy. Biological imperatives: survival, sex, social status
2. **More Complicated = Less Viral** — Describe in 3 words. Simple enough for 3-second TikTok demo
3. **"Did You Hear About This?"** — Remarkable products people naturally share. Create shareable moments
