# Key Points

## 6 Steps to First $100K

1. **Cut All Costs** — No eating out, cheapest housing, paid-off car
2. **Save Time** — Wake up 5-9 AM/PM for work on your business
3. **Research Skills** — Find what people already pay for
4. **Learn Skills** — Eliminate distractions, focus on learning
5. **Spend Money Right** — Tools, courses, trial attempts (ads, software)
6. **Don't Increase Lifestyle** — Keep expenses low even when income rises

## Key Quote

> "You want to be rich, not look rich. This is about $100K in the bank, not $100K in revenue."
