# Key Points

## 1. Find What to Build

**Forget passion. Find painful problems.**
- IKIGAI: intersection of what you love, good at, world needs, and will pay for
- Use AI to interview you: "I want to start a business. Interview me one question at a time."
- **A business starts the moment you sell to a stranger**

## 2. Build Your Offer

**4 Components:**
1. **Clear Promise** — Transformation, not features
2. **Guarantee** — Risk reversal
3. **Bonus** — Overcomes objections
4. **Scarcity** — Internal (cost of not acting)

**3 Price Points:** Anchor (5-10x) → Main → Cheaper version
**Rule:** Charge more than comfortable. Underpricing = terrible clients.

## 3. Find Buyers

- **Outreach is your job** (especially early)
- Use AI to build 50 personalized outreach/day
- Ask AI for prospects + contact info + personalized message

## 4. Sales Process

1. **Situation** — Understand where they feel blocked
2. **Model** — How you do what you do
3. **Offer** — Repeat back what they said
4. **Close** — "Based on what I shared, you'd be a perfect fit. What card did you want to put that on?"

**Practice with AI** — Act as skeptical buyer, push back, get objections

## 5. Deliver Value Fast

**Time to First Value (TTFV):** Shorten from days to minutes

With AI:
1. Create onboarding questionnaire
2. Send link immediately after pay
3. Build roadmap of transformation in Claude

## 6. Go Now

- Start small. Start messy.
- Every company started different than what ended up working.
- "Be patient with results, but wildly impatient with action."
