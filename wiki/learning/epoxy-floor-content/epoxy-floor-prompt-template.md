# EPOXY FLOORING PROMPTS — Frame to Video Template

Template để tạo nội dung TikTok video transformation bằng AI (construction/timelapse style).

**Công cụ:** ChatGPT + Flow AI (labs.google.com/flow) hoặc Runway, Kling, Sora, Veo 2

---

## CẤU TRÚC TỔNG QUAN

```
[10 Interior Ideas]
        ↓
[Chọn 1 room]
        ↓
[4 Image Prompts] ───→ [3 Video Prompts]
     ↑                       ↑
 Frame 1 ──→ Video 1 ──→ Frame 2
 Frame 2 ──→ Video 2 ──→ Frame 3
 Frame 3 ──→ Video 3 ──→ Frame 4
```

**QUAN TRỌNG:** Mỗi video prompt phải refer đến 2 frame tạo nên nó.

---

## QUY TẮC GLOBAL (Áp dụng cho TẤT CẢ prompts)

- Photorealistic, ultra-detailed, 8K quality
- Realistic construction workflow
- Camera always static (timelapse feel)
- Epoxy phải look như **poured liquid metallic marble patterns** (KHÔNG phải ceramic, marble, vinyl, flat paint)
- No text, logos, watermarks
- Color palette: phải match với room và style của Image prompts

---

## STEP 1: 10 INTERIOR IDEAS

Chọn 1 room từ danh sách dưới:

1. **Kitchen** — sàn bếp, tủ kệ, ánh sáng warm
2. **Bedroom** — phòng ngủ master, gạch men, ánh sáng soft
3. **Bathroom** — gạch ốp lát, ẩm ướt, ánh sáng neutral trắng
4. **Living Room** — phòng khách hiện đại, sofa, ánh sáng natural
5. **Dining Area** — phòng ăn, bàn ăn, ánh sáng warm dinner
6. **Garage** — sàn garage, ánh sáng industrial, tool rack
7. **Home Office** — phòng làm việc, bàn desk, ánh sáng focus
8. **Retail Shop** — cửa hàng, kệ trưng bày, ánh sáng spotlight
9. **Luxury Showroom** — showroom xe/nội thất cao cấp
10. **Studio Apartment** — căn hộ nhỏ, multi-functional space

## STEP 2: 4 IMAGE PROMPTS

**Cùng 1 room, cùng 1 góc camera, 4 giai đoạn:**

### Image 1: EMPTY / UNDER-CONSTRUCTION
```
[Góc camera cố định] của [room]. 
Empty room, bare concrete floor with cracks and stains. 
Exposed walls, no furniture. Harsh overhead lighting.
No装饰, no people. Realistic worn concrete texture.
```

### Image 2: MID-CONSTRUCTION (Workers Active)
```
[Góc camera] của [room]. 
2-3 workers in safety gear actively preparing floor.
Primer being applied. Dust particles in light beams.
Equipment visible: sander, roller, cleaning supplies.
Floor now clean but still unfinished concrete look.
```

### Image 3: COMPLETED EPOXY (The Transformation)
```
[Góc camera] của [room]. 
COMPLETED artistic metallic epoxy floor.
Liquid marble patterns with swirled pigments (chọn màu: silver/gold/copper/blue).
High-gloss mirror finish reflecting light.
No furniture yet. Room now transformed.
Poured liquid texture clearly visible — NOT ceramic, NOT vinyl.
```

### Image 4: FURNISHED FINAL
```
[Góc camera] của [room]. 
Same metallic epoxy floor now polished and gleaming.
Room fully furnished: [tùy room — sofa/bed/table].
Warm ambient lighting. Cohesive color scheme.
Final reveal shot showing transformation complete.
```

## STEP 3: 3 VIDEO PROMPTS (Frame-to-Video)

**QUY TẮC QUAN TRỌNG:** Mỗi video prompt phải refer đến:
1. **Start Frame** (Image trước đó)
2. **End Frame** (Image tiếp theo)
3. **Transition description** (cái gì xảy ra ở giữa)

---

### Video 1: EMPTY → MID-CONSTRUCTION

```
TRANSITION: Timelapse construction progress

START FRAME DESCRIPTION:
[Góc camera] của [room]. Empty room with bare concrete floor,
cracks and stains visible, exposed walls, no furniture,
harsh overhead lighting, dust particles in air.

END FRAME DESCRIPTION:  
[Góc camera] của [room]. Same room with 2-3 workers in
safety gear actively preparing floor. Primer being applied.
Dust being cleaned. Floor now clean and ready for epoxy.

VIDEO PROMPT:
"Cinematic timelapse transitioning from empty concrete floor
with visible cracks and stains to workers actively preparing
the surface for epoxy coating. Dust particles floating in light beams.
Sound of sanders and equipment. Realistic construction workflow.
Camera remains completely static at [gói camera từ Image 1].
Smooth, satisfying transformation. 8K cinematic quality."
```

---

### Video 2: MID-CONSTRUCTION → COMPLETED EPOXY

```
TRANSITION: Epoxy pour and cure (THE HERO SHOT)

START FRAME DESCRIPTION:
[Góc camera] của [room]. Workers finishing floor preparation.
Cleaned concrete surface ready for epoxy. Equipment being set aside.
Primer coat applied and dried. Room now quiet before the pour.

END FRAME DESCRIPTION:
[Góc camera] của [room]. Same room now features completed
artistic metallic epoxy floor with swirling [COLOR] pigments
creating liquid marble patterns. High-gloss mirror finish
reflecting overhead lights. No furniture. Transformed space.

VIDEO PROMPT:
"Smooth cinematic transition from floor preparation to liquid
metallic epoxy being carefully poured onto the surface. Pigments
in [COLOR] and [COLOR] swirling in slow motion creating natural
marble patterns. Camera static at [góc camera]. Liquid spreading
evenly across floor. Transition to slow-motion reveal of final
polished epoxy with mirror-like gloss. The transformation from
bare concrete to liquid marble masterpiece. 8K cinematic quality."
```

---

### Video 3: COMPLETED → FURNISHED

```
TRANSITION: Furniture reveal and ambient lighting

START FRAME DESCRIPTION:
[Góc camera] của [room]. Completed metallic epoxy floor with
swirling [COLOR] pigments, high-gloss finish, empty room,
no furniture, overhead lighting reflecting on polished surface.

END FRAME DESCRIPTION:
[Góc camera] của [room]. Same room now fully furnished with
[describe furniture: sofa, bed, table, chairs]. Warm ambient
lighting. Epoxy floor still gleaming with reflections.
Cohesive [modern/minimalist/luxury] aesthetic achieved.

VIDEO PROMPT:
"Cinematic reveal transitioning from empty transformed room
with gleaming epoxy floor to room now furnished with [furniture].
Warm ambient lighting turns on gradually. Furniture appearing
as if by magic — subtle fade from empty to furnished. Camera
static at [góc camera]. Final shot shows complete living space
with matching aesthetic. Epoxy floor remains the hero, now
complemented by warm lighting and furniture. 8K cinematic quality."
```
