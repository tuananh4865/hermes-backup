## His Viral App Framework (5-Part Series)

### Part 1: How to Find a Viral Idea

**3 Principles:**
1. **Solve big problems** — Maslow's hierarchy. Biological imperatives (survival, sex, status) = most viral
2. **More complicated = Less viral** — Can you describe it in 3 words?
3. **"Did you hear about this?"** — Remarkable products people share naturally

**3 Methods:**
1. **Scan social media** — Break out of your bubble. Create accounts for demographics you'd never engage with
2. **Research App Store** — Download every app in your space. Use SensorTower for revenue data
3. **Disconnect** — Best ideas come on walks, in showers. Your brain needs space to process inputs
