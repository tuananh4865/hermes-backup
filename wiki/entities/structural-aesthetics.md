## Video Analysis (from @Structural.Aesthetics)

### "Stunning Epoxy River Stone Floor with Suspended Bed"

**8-Stage Timelapse Structure:**

| Stage | Action | Workers |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Pour pebbles/mortar | ✅ 2 workers |
| 2 | Spread evenly | ✅ 2 workers |
| 3 | Pour epoxy/resin | ✅ 2 workers |
| 4 | Self-leveling | ❌ (无人) |
| 5 | Polish/final touches | ✅ 2 workers |
| 6 | Empty completed surface | ✅ 2 workers |
| 7 | Install floating bed | ✅ 2 workers |
| 8 | Final reveal | ❌ (无人) |

### Visual Elements

- **Workers:** Dark clothing (black, grey, navy), white caps, gloves
- **Decorative chain:** Black iron chain hanging from ceiling in EVERY frame
- **Lighting:** Natural window light from left
- **Camera:** Completely static wide-angle corner

### Why This Video Matters

This video became the reference for EPOXY FLOORING prompt template V4-V6 because:
1. Perfect 8-stage timelapse structure
2. Consistent worker identity throughout
3. No "magical" furniture appearance — everything installed with visible labor
4. Decorative chain provides visual continuity across all frames
