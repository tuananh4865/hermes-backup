# Dan Martell

SaaS founder. Known for building multiple SaaS businesses and exit at $46.2M.

## Background

- Former addict, went to rehab at 16
- Arrested twice, juvenile detention
- No money, no education
- Now: $42M distributions, $46.2M exit, $106M in a weekend

## His Key Insight

**The moment he felt richest: $100K in the bank (not his exits)**

> "Before that, I was sleeping on a gym floor and doing math on whether I could afford groceries. Once I had $100,000, I stopped having to worry about tomorrow."

## His 6 Steps to First $100K

1. **Cut all costs** — No eating out, cheapest housing, paid-off car
2. **Save time** — 5-9 AM/PM side business hours
3. **Research skills** — Find what people already pay for
4. **Learn skills** — Eliminate distractions, focus learning
5. **Spend money right** — Tools, courses, trial attempts
6. **Don't increase lifestyle** — Stay lean even when income rises

## Quote

> "You want to be rich, not look rich. This is about $100K in the bank, not $100K in revenue."
