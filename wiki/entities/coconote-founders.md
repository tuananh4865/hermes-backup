# Brett and Zach (CocoNote Founders)

Built **CocoNote** (formerly Coconut) — AI notetaker for students. Exit to Quizlet in 18 months.

## Stats

- 2M registered users
- $6.7M ARR
- $5M ARR within 1 year
- Exit to Quizlet in 18 months

## Their App Growth Framework

**Two things apps must cover:**
1. Repeatable use case
2. Core to identity

**Then** add wow factor on top.

## Key Lessons

### TikTok: Quality > Virality

> "We would rather have 10M views on a video that converts than 40M views that drives low-intent traffic."

**PDF to Brain Rot example:**
- 40M views
- Only $25K revenue
- Lesson: Virality ≠ revenue

### Navy Seals, Not Navy

Built team of 10-12 part-time creators who understood product + audience deeply.

### AI App Opportunities

- What's possible today that wasn't 6 months ago?
- Build for identity + repeat behavior
- Coconut mission: "Give learners superpowers"
- "If you have a phone in your pocket, you should be able to have a great tutor."
